Welcome!

I'm Imani Mclemore, an aspiring graphic and UI/UX designer. I want to create remarkable designs and do the things that I'm passionate about.

I always had a passion for design because it inspires me to have my designs on an item that will catch someone's eye. I want someone to look at my design and say "Hey! This is really cool! I wonder who made it", so I can reply with "Me!". Just the feeling of inspiring other artists and making people happy with my designs brings a warm feeling to my heart.
